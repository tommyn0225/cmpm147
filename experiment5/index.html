Description

This experiment evolves abstract impressions of a selected image. Choose Pikachu, the Google Chrome logo, or the "Iwo Jima" photo, then watch a 10×10 rectangle grid sampled from that image evolve via Gaussian mutations. Drag the mutation slider from 100% to 1% over ~30 seconds to anneal your result.

Technical

Built with p5.js: initDesign() downsamples the image, renderDesign() draws semi-transparent boxes, and mutateDesign() applies Gaussian noise to each box’s size, position, and tone. Scores are computed by pixel-wise MSE and used to snapshot top designs.

Reflection

It was challenging to fine‑tune the generator so that each evolution remained abstract yet still recognizable Striking the right balance between grid resolution and mutation strength required a decent amount of testing
