Description

This experiment evolves abstract impressions of a selected image. Choose Pikachu, the Chrome logo, or the iconic Iwo Jima photo, then watch a 10×10 rectangle grid sampled from that image evolve via Gaussian mutations. Drag the mutation slider from 100% to 1% over ~30 seconds to anneal your result.

Technical

Built with p5.js: initDesign() downsamples the image, renderDesign() draws semi-transparent boxes, and mutateDesign() applies Gaussian noise to each box’s size, position, and tone. Scores are computed by pixel-wise MSE and used to snapshot top designs.

Reflection

Tuning grid resolution and mutation σ was key; too coarse or too noisy and evolution either missed detail or failed to converge. Human-guided annealing finds balance between exploration and refinement.
